1. No Personal Data Collection

MiniMinds is a free children's learning application designed to foster educational engagement without the need for data collection. We firmly believe in protecting the privacy of our users, especially children, and as such, the Application does not collect, store, or share any personal data from its users.

2. No Advertisements

To provide a safe and distraction-free learning environment, MiniMinds operates without displaying any advertisements. This approach ensures a focus on educational content, free from unnecessary interruptions or data collection related to advertising.

3. Use of the Application

The Application is designed to be used as is, without the need for creating user accounts or providing personal information. Users can access all features of the Application without compromising their privacy.
